Project Alpha

A high-octane 3D space combat simulator built in Unity.

About the Game

Project Alpha is a fast-paced 3D space combat simulator. Featuring procedural skyboxes, advanced AI wingmen, and intense dogfighting mechanics. Players navigate through asteroid fields and engage in large-scale fleet battles.

Gameplay Highlights

What I Did

• Space Flight Model: Developed a Newtonian-based flight system with intuitive controls.
• AI Systems: Programmed complex flocking and state-machine behavior for enemy and allied ships.
• Visual Effects: Created custom shaders for warp drive effects and shield impacts.
