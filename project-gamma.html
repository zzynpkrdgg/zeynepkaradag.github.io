Project Gamma

An atmospheric top-down survival horror set in a decaying research facility.

About the Game

Project Gamma is a tense top-down survival horror experience. Players navigate the dark corridors of a forgotten lunar base, managing limited resources while avoiding terrifying extraterrestrial threats. The game emphasizes atmosphere and careful planning over direct combat.

Gameplay Highlights

What I Did

• Dynamic Lighting: Implemented a performant 2D lighting system to enhance the horror atmosphere.
• Fog of War: Developed a visibility system that obscures parts of the map based on the player's line of sight.
• Sound Design: Integrated a spatial audio system to provide immersive environmental cues.
